Matt Ritter

Echoes

Spring 2022

Software/Materials Used:
Cinema 4D, After Effects, Blown Glass, Stable Diffusion
In this piece, I attempt to showcase the optical properties of glass as a material. After applying a mirror to the backside of the glass, I render several videos in Cinema 4D, and then distort them using polar coordinates in After Effects. Then, I project the distorted video onto the cylindrical mirror. Made at UrbanGlass in Brooklyn, New York.
